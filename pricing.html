Common Questions

What counts as a "saving"?

Anything measurable. Time saved on manual tasks, reduced error rates, fewer staff hours on repetitive work, faster turnaround on deliverables. We agree the metrics upfront during the audit so there are no surprises.

What if AI isn't the right fit for my business?

Then we'll tell you. We've turned work away before because it wasn't the right call. We'd rather be honest than take money for something that won't deliver. You'll still walk away with useful insights from the consultation.

How long before I see results?

Most businesses see measurable improvements within weeks, not months. We start with the quick wins — the things that make an immediate difference — and build from there.

Is there a minimum contract length?

We keep things flexible. The subscription runs as long as the tools are delivering value. If you ever feel it's not working, we'll have an honest conversation about it.

We're a large organisation. Does this model still work?

Absolutely. The percentage-of-savings model scales naturally. For larger operations, the savings tend to be bigger, the baseline is more robust, and the ROI is even clearer.
